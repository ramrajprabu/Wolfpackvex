First drivable working version. Controls are as follows:
A & C for drivetrain - motors 2 & 8
L for lift - motors 3 & 9
R for intake - motors 1 & 7
Port 5 - bottom bumper
Port 11 - top bumper
Port 4 - TouchLED for switching autonomous segments
Port 10 - TouchLed for restarting/stopping autonomous

50 is middle of axes A, B, C, and D on controller
