First drivable working version. Controls are as follows:
A & C for drivetrain - motors 5 & 11
L for lift - motors 10 & 4
R for intake - motors 6 & 12
Port 9 - bottom bumper
Port 3 - top bumper
Port 8 - TouchLED for switching autonomous segments
Port 2 - TouchLed for restarting/stopping autonomous

50 is middle of axes A, B, C, and D on controller
